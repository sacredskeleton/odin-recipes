Lasagne recipe.

What is Lasagne?

In simple, lasagne is a dish based on pasta, cheese and meat. This dish is easy to make, cheap, yummy and very filling!

This dish is very flavourful with a wide range of ingredients that most have in their home already!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus a little for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • Tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • 300g pack fresh lasagne sheets
  • White sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella, torn into thin strips

Instructions:

  1. Saute: Heat the oil in a deep soup pot. Add the onion, garlic, cauliflower, cashews, and turmeric. Saute for 10 minutes or until very fragrant.
  2. Simmer: Add the water (Starting with 4 cups) and salt. Simmer until softened
  3. Blend: Transfer the soup to a blender and blend into a creamyconsistancy. It's best to do this in batches.
  4. Finish: Return to the soup pot and add any additional water (remaining 2-3 cups) if you need to thin it out. Also a good time to season with more salt and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Finally, top with spiced chickpeas and parsley and olive oil alongisde some more salt and put on your most comfy sweater and enjoy!
