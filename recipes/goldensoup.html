What is golden soup?

Golden soup is a very healthy soup known for its beneficial properties such as reducing inflammation, boosting antioxidants, improving skin quality, settling upset stomachs or cramps and overall being very good for your body.

This dish is very flavourful with a wide range of ingredients that most have in their home already! The real beauty of this dish is due to the fact you can add or remove anything you wish!
