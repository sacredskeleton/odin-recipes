What is chilli con carne?

Chilli con carne is a really easy dish. It offers loads of flavour even though it mostly uses store cupboard ingredients. You can add or remove spice at your own level by leaving the seeds in the fresh chillies or adding a pinch of dried chilli flakes!

Although this dish is well known to be spicy, if that's not your cuppa, then feel free to remove the spice! Each serving is usually 420 calories. So it's quite a light dish!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1kg lean beef mince
  • 250ml red wine (Optional)
  • 800g chopped tomato
  • 3 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 2 red chillies, thinky sliced.
  • 1 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoons coriander
  • 1 cinnamon stick or half a teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • good shake of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 800g red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large bunch fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Lime wedges, rice, guacamole, soured cream and green salad to serve

Instructions:

  1. Saute: Heat the oil in a deep soup pot. Add the onion, garlic, cauliflower, cashews, and turmeric. Saute for 10 minutes or until very fragrant.
  2. Simmer: Add the water (Starting with 4 cups) and salt. Simmer until softened
  3. Blend: Transfer the soup to a blender and blend into a creamyconsistancy. It's best to do this in batches.
  4. Finish: Return to the soup pot and add any additional water (remaining 2-3 cups) if you need to thin it out. Also a good time to season with more salt and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Finally, top with spiced chickpeas and parsley and olive oil alongisde some more salt and put on your most comfy sweater and enjoy!
